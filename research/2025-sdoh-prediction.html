Social Determinants of Health Prediction for ICD-9 Code with Reasoning Models

Read on OpenReview
Sharim Khan
ML4H 2025
2025
EHR / ICD Codes

Abstract

Social determinants of health (SDOH) — including housing instability, food insecurity, and transportation barriers — are increasingly recognized as critical drivers of health outcomes but remain systematically under-documented in clinical records. This paper investigates whether reasoning-capable language models can predict SDOH factors from ICD-9 coded diagnoses alone, without access to free-text notes. We benchmark several reasoning model architectures and find that chain-of-thought prompting enables meaningful SDOH inference from structured codes, offering a scalable path to SDOH screening in settings where clinical notes are unavailable or de-identified.
