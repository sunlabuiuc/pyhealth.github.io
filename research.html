About the Initiative

We welcome anyone passionate about advancing computational healthcare — regardless of career stage or institutional affiliation. Participants work on innovative projects that lead to publications, open-source software, and real clinical impact. Our work is grounded in four core tenets.

📊

Reproducible

Every result ships with open code, standardized pipelines, and clear documentation so others can verify, build on, and extend our findings.

🧩

Extensible

Research contributions integrate directly into PyHealth, making them reusable building blocks for the broader healthcare AI community.

🔓

Accessible

Open to all — students, engineers, clinicians, and independent researchers. No title or institutional affiliation required to contribute.

🩺

Clinically Relevant

Projects tackle real problems in healthcare — clinical decision support, risk stratification, rare disease, and more — in partnership with domain experts.
